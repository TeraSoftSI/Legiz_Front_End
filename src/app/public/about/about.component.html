About Us

TeraSoft is a company created in order to offer digital solutions to problems and needs that arise in the contemporary world.

For this purpose, Legiz is developed in this report.

This project is a web application created with the purpose of serving as a platform that provides a communication channel between lawyers and their clients to provide solutions to legal problems, in addition to having the opportunity to provide legal advice.

In this way, Legiz aims to be a meeting point between lawyers and people who have legal problems or need legal advice of any kind.
